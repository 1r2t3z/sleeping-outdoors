How many nights?

The first question to ask yourself is how many nights you plan to spend sleeping outdoors. Are you looking for a quick weekend getaway or a longer expedition? This will determine the type of equipment you'll need and the level of comfort you desire.



Which part of the year?

The time of year plays a crucial role in planning your outdoor sleeping experience. Are you a fan of warm summer nights or do you prefer the crisp air of autumn? Consider the weather conditions, temperature fluctuations, and any potential challenges that may arise during different seasons.



How much are you willing to spent?

Budget is an important factor to consider when deciding on your outdoor sleeping arrangements. Are you willing to invest in high-quality gear for long-term use, or are you looking for more affordable options? Keep in mind that investing in durable equipment can enhance your overall experience and provide better comfort and safety.
